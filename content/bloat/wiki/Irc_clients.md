Irc clients
===========

-   Linux: pidgin or empathy work well, as does xchat
-   Mac: pidgin or audium
-   Windows: pidgin

Libotr is recomended, too.

The developers hang out primarily on the \#bufferbloat and \#openwrt
channels on chat.freenode.net
